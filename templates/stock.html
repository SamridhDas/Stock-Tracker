CandleStick Chart Analysis

Candlestick charts are one of the most widely used tools in technical analysis for understanding stock price movement over time. Each candle represents a specific time period and displays four key pieces of information: the opening price, closing price, highest price, and lowest price. Green candles typically indicate that the stock closed higher than it opened, while red candles indicate a decline in price during that period. By analyzing candle patterns, traders and investors can identify market trends, momentum, volatility, and potential reversal signals to make more informed investment decisions.
